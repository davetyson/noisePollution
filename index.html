Howdy Rock 'n' Rollers! Welcome to Noise Pollution Rock School

Do you want to learn the basics of guitar, bass, singing and drums in a fun, easy and approachable way? At Noise Pollution Rock School it’s more about getting kids excited about playing music and less about the boring formalities, because in Rock ‘n’ Roll, there are no rules!

Classes are 45 minutes and for ages 7 and up. Located on Hamilton mountain in my attic studio. No experience or instruments are required! We supply everything! I believe music is a tool for self discovery and creative expression. So let’s learn, grow, make friends and most importantly… Let’s Rock!!!

- Matt Ellis

Classes

  • Class Details

    • Class duration is 45 minutes, Monday-Thursday at both 5pm and 6pm

    • 2-4 kids per class learning guitar, bass, drums, and singing

    • Kids will be jamming and learning rock and pop songs

    • Each 12-week term is $240 per child (to accommodate kids joining mid-term, please contact me directly)

    • We offer trial jams and weekend "drop-ins" or "booster lessons" if a student is seeking one-on-one instruction

    • Birthday party jams and performances are also available!
